SESSION STRUCTURE

A Routine, Not a Random Click-Fest

01

Warm Up — 3 min

Run Flickshot first. Low target count, focus on clean first-shot accuracy over speed. This wakes up your mouse arm before anything fast.

02

Build Control — 4 min

Move to Tracking. Smooth, low-sensitivity correction. This is where most Valorant whiffs actually come from — not the flick, the follow-through.

03

Apply In Context — 5 min

Finish on Peek Reaction or Target Switch. These mirror real duels — pre-aim, react, clear. Bring this sharpness straight into your ranked queue.
